The "Radius Gap" Mystery

One of the most fascinating things about sub-Neptunes is a phenomenon known as the Fulton Gap (or radius valley). Astronomers noticed a strange scarcity of planets that are exactly 1.5 to 2 times the size of Earth.
- Planets just below this gap are usually rocky super-Earths.
- Planets just above this gap are gaseous sub-Neptunes.
